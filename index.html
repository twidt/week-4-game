Crystal Gem Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystal gems to the right. By clicking on a crystal gem you will add a specific amount of point to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal gem is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal gem.
